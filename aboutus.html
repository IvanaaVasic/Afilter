A FILTER
Part of a family
First of our companies, Alex Company, was founded over 20 years ago, as one of the first companies in Serbia who was distributing state of the art water filtration systems.

Around 10 years ago, we have formed a sister company Alex Engineering & Construction, which was focused in architecture, construction works ad spa and wellness centers development. With the experience gathered over the years, we have positioned ourselves as one of the most prominent companies in the field of designing and building commercial swimming pools.

Manufacture of our own equipment was a logical step.
WE MAKE FILTERS
And they are Great
We are proud manufacturers of a premium quality, fully customizable GRP sand filters.

Being in a swimming pool industry for almost 20 years, we have developed our own brand of sand filters – A FILTER. We manufacture them in our own factory in Belgrade. All of our filters are designed in-house by our own R&D team.

A FILTER is a subsidiary of ALEX E&C dedicated to development and manufacture of high
quality GRP sand filters.
